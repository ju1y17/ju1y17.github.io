    About the Essential Cs.

    We are the exporters of fresh and quality fruits, vegetables and food crops from Uganda, East Africa. The Essential Cs is a Uganda based company that specializes in the production and export of clean fruits, vegetables and food crops from Uganda. It is also deeply rooted on social entrepreneurship through providing employment opportunities to Ugandans and providing all year round market for farmer's produce.

    Who we are?

    We are exporters of fresh tropical fruits and vegetables from Uganda. We work with a large farmers network across the country to ensure availability of quality products through out the year.

    Discover More

    What we do?

    We work with a large base of farmers and agricultural experts across the country and these help to form the company's supply base of produce. Working closely with the experts and the network of farmers, we encourage and promote the use of clean planting material together with the necessary experts approved agronomical practices. In the long run, we offer social entrepreneural services in terms of providing employment to both the agricultural experts, the carefully selected farmers and also to the team that provides cleaning and sorting,packaging and transportation services.
